How to charge your EV with VoltSense

Scan · Pay · Plug in · Done

Your charging session in 7 steps

1
📷
Scan the QR code
Find the VoltSense QR sticker on the charger. Scan it with your phone camera. No app download needed.
2
🔋
Select your car and battery level
Choose your car model and enter your current battery %. We'll calculate exactly how much you need and show you what percentage you'll reach after charging.
3
⚡
Choose a charging package
Pick Smart Fill (recommended — charges exactly what your car needs), a fixed kWh package, or enter a custom amount. All prices are shown before you pay.
4
📱
Pay via GCash, Maya, or card
Tap your preferred payment method. You'll be redirected to a secure PayMongo payment page. Your card or GCash/Maya wallet is charged immediately.
5
🔌
Plug in your car
After payment is confirmed, plug the charging cable into your car. The charger activates automatically within 30 seconds. You do NOT need to press any button on the charger.
6
📊
Watch the progress
The page updates live — showing kWh delivered and estimated time remaining. You'll see your battery % rising in real time.
7
✅
Charging stops automatically
When your package kWh limit is reached, the charger stops automatically. Unplug and you're done. If you charged less than expected, the unused amount is refunded to your original payment method within 3–5 banking days.
